WHY US?
Strong Project Management
One of benefits of using an external company like Tribe Coders is ability to work closely with experienced Product Owners.
 
Product Owner is involved into requirements definition when a project starts. His routine responsibilities are project planing, team management, early project risk definition / elimination and reporting to client. All projects are under CTO coordination.
 
 
HOW WE WORK?
 
STEPS
 
Customer submits to Tribe Coders a request for proposal with project reuirements.
 
 
Product Owner analyses it and provides the client with a appropriate proposal that describes the process for implementing the requirements and budget estimates.
 
 
Once the proposal is accepted, Tribe Coders assigns a project team, completes the necessary documentation and initiates the development process.
 
 
We use Agile/SCRUM with extensive QA & testing to deliver superior results.
 
 
Once the project is coded and tested, Tribe Coders releases the project to the customer.
 
 
Product Owner collects all comments and if necessary start the next iteration of the development process.
 
 
Tribe Coders deliver the final product.
