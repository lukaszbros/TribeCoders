MGMT is a mobile application that allows club owners to offer special events to their members (such as golf courses, boat trips, festivals of speed, etc.). The App has a back office configuration panel that allows adding, deleting and modifying customers and events.
 
The frontend part is pure HTML5, wrapped up with PhoneGap. Its backend is based on Ruby on Rails.
