MGMT is a mobile application that allows club members owners to offer special events to their members (such as golf courses, boat trips, festivals of speed, etc). App has a backoffice configuration panel that allows to add, delete and modify customers and events.
 
Frontend part is pure HTML5, wrapped up with PhoneGap. Backend in Ruby on Rails.
